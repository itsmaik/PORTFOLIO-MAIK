About me

More about me

I’ve always been fascinated by technology and its potential to improve our lives. That's why my biggest goal is to develop human-centered technological solutions that truly matter.

I started out in aviation operations, leading teams and managing day-to-day processes at an airport - an experience that taught me a lot about people, structure, and staying calm under pressure. Over time, that passion for solving problems evolved and led me into technology, where I saw the opportunity to make a difference on a larger scale.

After completing my Front-End Development degree at Noroff, I’m now studying IT: Front-End and Mobile Development at Kristiania University College, where I’m building a broader foundation in full-stack and mobile development - creating APIs, working with databases, and developing native apps for iOS and Android.

I’d describe myself as communicative, creative, curious, open-minded, and a strong team player who’s always eager to learn and grow.
